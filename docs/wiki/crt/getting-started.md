# Getting Started with Ash-Dash

Welcome to Ash-Dash, the Crisis Response Team dashboard for [The Alphabet Cartel](https://discord.gg/alphabetcartel) community. This guide will walk you through logging in and navigating the dashboard.

## Logging In

Ash-Dash uses **PocketID** for authenticationâ€”the same system you use for other Alphabet Cartel services.

### First-Time Login

1. Navigate to the Ash-Dash URL (provided by your CRT lead)
2. Click **"Sign in with PocketID"**
3. Enter your PocketID credentials
4. Grant Ash-Dash permission to access your profile
5. You'll be redirected to the dashboard

### Subsequent Logins

Your session persists for 24 hours. If you're logged out:

1. Visit the Ash-Dash URL
2. You'll be automatically redirected to PocketID
3. Confirm your login (or enter credentials if session expired)
4. Return to where you left off

### Troubleshooting Login

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| "Unauthorized" error | Contact CRT leadershipâ€”you may not be in the CRT group |
| Login loop | Clear browser cookies and try again |
| PocketID down | Wait and retry; check Discord for announcements |

## Quick Actions

### Viewing a Session

1. Click any session in the Active Sessions panel
2. Or navigate to **Sessions** and click a row

### Refreshing Data

- Data auto-refreshes every 10 seconds (sessions) and 30 seconds (metrics)
- Click the **Refresh** button for immediate update
- The "Live" indicator shows real-time polling is active

## Understanding Session States

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Active** | Session is open and needs monitoring |
| **Closed** | Session resolved, CRT intervention complete |
| **Archived** | Session encrypted and moved to long-term storage |
