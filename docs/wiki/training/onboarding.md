## Understanding Ash-Dash

### Key Sections

| Section | What's There |
|---------|--------------|
| **Dashboard** | Active sessions, metrics, charts |
| **Sessions** | Session list, search, filters, history |
| **Archives** | Encrypted long-term storage (CRT Lead+) |
| **Wiki** | All documentation and training materials |

### Session Workflow

```
Alert â†’ Review â†’ Respond â†’ Document â†’ Close
```

1. **Alert**: Ash detects potential crisis (appears on Dashboard)
2. **Review**: Check context, history, and NLP analysis
3. **Respond**: Reach out to community member via Discord
4. **Document**: Write session notes as you work
5. **Close**: Mark session resolved when crisis passes

## When to Escalate

Escalate to CRT Leadership when:

- Immediate danger to self or others
- You feel out of your depth
- The situation involves minors
- Legal concerns arise
- You need support

**It's always okay to ask for help.**
